About ADE

ADE stands for APML Development Engine - a revolutionary system that lets anyone build professional applications without coding.

APML is the Agent Protocol Markup Language, created by Tom Cassidy in 2025 as a series of protocols to make building with AI agents more effective.

The Story Behind APML

üè• The Chinese Hospital Inspiration

In January 2020, during the COVID-19 outbreak in Wuhan, China achieved something impossible: they built a fully functional 1,000-bed hospital in just 10 days. How?

Not by working faster, but by working smarter:

  • Multiple teams built different parts simultaneously in different locations
  • Every connection point was defined precisely before construction began
  • Hundreds of specialists worked in parallel without conflicts
  • Perfect coordination made the impossible possible

APML applies this same principle to software: Multiple AI agents working in perfect coordination to build your application at unprecedented speed.

How ADE Works

ADE uses the APML framework to orchestrate multiple AI agents, each specializing in different aspects of your application:

  1. THINK - You describe what you want in plain language
  2. SEE - ADE shows you what it understood and plans to build
  3. BUILD - Multiple AI teams work in parallel to create your app
  4. FEEL - You choose how your app looks and feels
  5. DEPLOY - Your app goes live, ready for users
